About Me

I am Pablo De La Garza, an adaptable professional transitioning from architecture to web development. With a Bachelor of Architecture degree and 8 years of experience in design and project management, I bring a unique blend of creativity, technical proficiency, and problem-solving skills to the field. Proficient in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React, I create visually appealing and interactive websites.


My architectural background enhances my eye for aesthetics and understanding of user experience. With strong attention to detail and problem-solving abilities, I ensure optimal outcomes. Collaborative and communicative, I thrive in multidisciplinary teams. I combine my design sensibilities with technical expertise to deliver exceptional web experiences.


Eager to contribute to the dynamic world of web development, I embrace continuous growth and lifelong learning.
